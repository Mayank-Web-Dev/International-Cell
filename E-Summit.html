TRACK 3

Forensic Psychology

•Assessing potential biases in forensic evidence that may affect convictions.

•Examining the ethical implications of forensic psychologists serving dual roles.

TRACK 4

Eco-Anxiety

•Analysing the causes,manifestations, and impacts of eco-anxiety on mental health among individuals and communities.
