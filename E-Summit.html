About The

E-SUMMIT

Holistic mental health solutions: addressing contemporary issues in diverse contexts

The virtual e-Summit on Holistic Mental Health Solutions brings international students together to explore and address contemporary mental health challenges. Themed "Holistic Mental Health Solutions: Addressing Contemporary Issues in Diverse Contexts," it features four parallel tracks focused on solution-oriented approaches and policy development.

Designed to foster dialogue and networking, the e-Summit provides a platform for students from diverse backgrounds to engage in meaningful discussions. Through collaborative sessions and knowledge exchange, the event promotes the development of resilient communities and enhances mental health outcomes globally. By focusing on comprehensive solutions and fostering international dialogue, the e-Summit seeks to shape the future of mental health care.

TRACK 1

Provisions of Mental Health in Education, Workplace, and Insurance

•Examining mental health provisions in educational institutions, including leave policies and credit system adjustments for students facing mental health challenges.

•Analysing the impact of corporate reimbursement practices that require diagnostic disclosures, breaching privacy and confidentiality.

•Discussing the limitations of health insurance policies that do not cover mental health services and explore potential reforms

TRACK 2

Technology and Mental Health Accessibility

•Discussing how technology,including telemedicine and digital health platforms,transforms access to mental health services.

•Exploring challenges such as the digital divide, privacy concerns, and the effectiveness of digital interventions.

•Highlighting innovative technological solutions that enhance mental health support, particularly in underserved and remote areas.

TRACK 3

Forensic Psychology

•Assessing potential biases in forensic evidence that may affect convictions.

•Examining the ethical implications of forensic psychologists serving dual roles.

•Discussing the complexities of competency and insanity evaluations in legal contexts.

TRACK 4

Eco-Anxiety

•Analysing the causes,manifestations, and impacts of eco-anxiety on mental health among individuals and communities.

•Exploring how psychological insights can help address eco- anxiety and promote mental resilience in environmental challenges.

•Analysing instances of green hypocrisy and discussing how psychological theories can inform our understanding of this phenomenon.

•Examining the role of policies, regulations, and corporate responsibility in mitigating eco- anxiety and promoting sustainable practices.
